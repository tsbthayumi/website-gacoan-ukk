About Us

Mie Gacoan

"Mie Gacoan" adalah sebuah merk dagang dari jaringan restaurant mie pedas No. 1 di Indonesia, yang menjadi anak perusahaan PT Pesta Pora Abadi. Berdiri sejak awal tahun 2016, saat ini merk "Mie Gacoan" telah tumbuh menjadi market leader F&B terbesar di Indonesia. Mengusung konsep bersantap modern dengan harga yang affordable, kehadiran "Mie Gacoan" telah mendapatkan apresiasi luar biasa di setiap market dimana "Mie Gacoan" hadir untuk melayani puluhan ribu pelanggan setiap bulannya. Oleh karena itu, inovasi akan selalu dikedepankan agar "Mie Gacoan" tetap relevan dan menjadi pilihan terbaik bagi para customer loyal.

Gacoan History

  • 2016: Mie Gacoan was established in Malang, East Java. The brand quickly gained popularity for its spicy noodle dishes and unique marketing approach.

  • 2017: Harris Kristanto joined the company as the Human Resource Director (HRD), though there was a common misconception that he was the owner​​.

  • 2022: By the end of this year, Mie Gacoan expanded significantly, employing over 3,000 people across numerous outlets primarily in Java, Sumatra, and Bali​​.

  • November 2022: Mie Gacoan obtained a halal certification from LPPOM MUI, which prompted a rebranding of some menu items to meet halal certification standards​​.

  • February 2023: The names of the menu items were changed from those with demonic or devilish connotations to more neutral names to comply with halal certification​
